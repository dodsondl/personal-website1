What I Offer




Development
With technology changing immensely rapidly, it is important to keep an up to date website with the accurate programming behind it. I am experienced in front-end development with mainly HTML, CSS, Javascript and Jquery. Currently, I am in the process of learning Swift to be able to make mobile IOS applications on Xcode.
Design
From wireframe mockups to the graphic design in photoshop and illustrator, I will be able to help with the overall design of the website. I believe that the little things like color choice and typography can make or break a website. It is essential to create a beautiful website that is simple and functional.
User Experience (UX)
Having a website with poor usability can anger and turn off potential customers. I believe that companies should be involved in usability testing to create a website that is easy for the visitor to navigate and find what they're looking for. Understanding personas can lead to a better designed and more versatile website.
